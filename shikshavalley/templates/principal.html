PRINCIPAL'S DESK
Dear Friends
Greetings to all
Welcome to Shiksha Valley Public School, Delhi. I am happy to accompany you in the Educational Journey through the various departments, activities, scholastic and non scholastic life of our children who are entrusted to us by the parents. Our methodology of education is not to produce little academic monsters, dehumanized, nor is it to produce pious faithful, allergic to the world they live in, incapable of responding to it sympathetically; but rather to produce balanced, serene, and constant individuals open to whatever is human; men and women of right principles; men and women open to the signs of the times, in tune with their cultural milieu and its problems; and above all men and women for others. Unfortunately, in today’s society children’s minds and hearts are being silently and gradually destroyed in the mindless race for reaching the top in the shortest time. Learning dissociated from the inculcation of virtues and values will produce only a hybrid of intellectual giants and moral dwarves. The value system which the school professes is incorporated in the regular curriculum in order to build up right attitude towards self, towards others, society and towards different cultures.
This objective is expressed through various activities of the school. Bringing out the best in every child occupies a prime position in our school education. The children are trained to be men and women with human as well as spiritual values to lead their lives. The children are motivated and moulded to become beacons shedding light all around. They learn to be good human beings with a positive attitude towards life, to have respect for honesty, integrity and commitment to others. They are made to realize that they are very important and all their actions affect the society and vice versa. This creates a sense of responsibility among the students. All these help the children to identify and appreciate the uniqueness in every one, thus enabling everyone to stand among the crowd as independent, respected individuals.
The role of parents is an essential factor in shaping the future of the children. The cooperation of the parents with the school management, and the staff will go a long way to make our children genuine human beings.
I wish all our students and the parents, and all the staff members, both the teaching and the non teaching, God’s blessings.
Sr. Vipul Gupta
